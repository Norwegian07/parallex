NO.1:- West Lake (Xi Hu)

At No.1 we have West Lake (Xi Hu) which is most visited by many people around the world every year. It is a fresh lake in Hangzhou, China. There are numerous temples, pagodas(Asian tiered towers), gardens, and natural/artificial Islands within the lake. This lake influenced poets and painters throughout Chinese history. It was made a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 2011.
We can go on a ride in this lake and another interesting thing is we can rent a bike and experience the beauty of this location. There is curious mythology in this place known as White Snake, better you don't miss the places that are interlinked to this mythology.
We can experience many other aspects of this lake So never miss this place, It is good for both couples, families and solo travelers.
